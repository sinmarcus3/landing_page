About us

What we do.

We build solutions that help our clients and customers meet their energy needs, more effectively and efficiently than alternative options in the market.

We combine industry experience with practical insights from the technology space to identify appropriate solutions that enable our clients to better manage their energy resources and supply.

We work with a wide network of stakeholders, across different industry sectors in New Zealand. This includes property companies, energy retailers, generators, lines companies, residential, commercial and industrial consumers.

Co.Energy Services

We Work With You
To Generate Results That Matter.

Energy Management

We help our clients' manage their energy resources by finding solutions that reduce energy use, improve energy efficiency or reduces their overall energy costs.

Network Services

We help set up, maintain and manage our clients' networks by profiling load and modelling constraints to optimise distribution across the network.

Distributed Energy

We help our clients tackle distributed energy systems and resources by undertaking feasibillity studies, commercial applications and identifying different ways to optimise value.

Data Analytics & Modelling

We help our clients find value by using our technical capabilities and experience in analysis and modelling to identify potential opportunities and value.

Energy Supply & Procurement

We support the energy procurement and tendering process through research, analysis and modelling support to help our clients' engage with their energy suppliers and partners.

Energy Consulting

We provide consultancy services on energy strategy, asset management, energy transactions and infrastructure, and regulatory and policy changes.
